Drinks


At our café, we’re proud to offer three distinctive drinks that are as unique as they are delicious. Start with our Espresso, a rich and bold shot of coffee that delivers a pure, intense flavour in every sip. And if you're craving something refreshing, our Iced Coffee is the perfect choice – a chilled, invigorating brew served over ice, ideal for those warm days or when you need a cool pick-me-up. Whether you're in the mood for a classic espresso or something more adventurous, we've got a drink to suit every taste.
